Frequently Asked Questions

How does crypto mining work on your platform?

We operate large-scale mining facilities with the latest ASIC miners. When you purchase a mining plan, you're allocated a portion of our total hashrate. Your earnings are calculated based on your share of the mining power and paid out automatically.

What happens to my real estate tokens if the property sells?

When a property is sold, the proceeds are distributed proportionally to all token holders after deducting any expenses. You'll receive your share in cryptocurrency to your connected wallet.

How are pharmaceutical research projects selected?

Our medical advisory board reviews all potential research projects based on scientific merit, potential impact, and likelihood of FDA approval. Only the top 5% of submissions make it to our platform.

What cryptocurrencies do you accept for investments?

We currently accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USD Coin (USDC), and our native CSPH token. All investments are denominated in USD equivalent value at time of purchase.

How are investment returns taxed?

Tax treatment varies by jurisdiction. In most countries, mining rewards are treated as income at receipt, while capital gains apply when you sell appreciated assets. We provide annual tax statements to help with reporting.

What security measures protect my investments?

We use multi-signature wallets, cold storage for 95% of assets, regular security audits, and insurance coverage. All smart contracts are thoroughly vetted before deployment.
